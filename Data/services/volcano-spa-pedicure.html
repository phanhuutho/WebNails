VOLCANO SPA PEDICURE (7 step)

This is new luxury spa experience in the single-use pedicure treatment. Detox volcano crystals and activations combine to create an invigorating and detoxifying experience with a fun bubbling explosion! The Pedicure treatment is then complete with an exfoliating sugar scrub, collagen cream mask with collagen lotion made with organic ingredients. Including Our Special treatment neck warmer and hot stone massage then finish with Paraffin wax to make your relaxing to the next level.

Step 1:
Detox Volcano Crystals

To prepare for the fun bubble explosion, first add the full packet of Volcano Crystals to warm spa water. Be sure to spread the volcano crystals evenly throughout.

Step 2:
Detox Volcano Activator

Immediately add the full packet of Volcano Activator to begin the bubbling Volcano eruption. Clients will especially enjoy this effect as the entire room fills with the lovely fragrance! Allow feet to soak for approximately three minutes as the reaction detoxifies and softens the skin. Dry legs and feet with a towel. To blend the ingredients and boost the fragrance, turn on the water jets before continuing.

Step 3:
Exfoliating Sugar Scrub

Apply a generous amount of sugar scrub and massage gently to exfoliate the skin. These raw sugars and essential oils help renew the texture of skin and enhance vibrancy, leaving skin feeling soft and smooth. Be sure to rinse the sugar scrub and towel dry.

Step 4:
Collagen Cream Maske

The Collagen cream mask is formulated to draw out any remaining impurities while rejuvenating skin’s appearance. Apply Mask evenly over legs and feet, then wrap a warm towel around both legs. Allow the mask to dry for 10 minutes to provide the full benefits. Rinse both legs afterwards and towel dry.

Step 5:
Collagen Massage Lotion

For silky soft results, massage lotion onto legs and feet until it has been completely absorbed. This final step soothes and moisturizes, leaving your client’s skin feeling hydrated all day.
